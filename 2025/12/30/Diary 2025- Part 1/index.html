Summary and Review of 2025 Part 1

Created2025-12-30|Updated2026-01-01|【Wilderness & Leading】-旷野与带领
|Post Views:

As 2025 comes to an end,
I realize that the true turning point of my life began in the second half of 2024.
Yet for me, 2025 became an entirely new path.

This year reshaped how I see God
and how I understand my own life.
Someone entered my life—
my loved one, my soulmate,
and my closest friend.

Through his presence,
I began to experience God’s love not as an abstract idea,
but as something tangible, abundant,
and capable of holding my life.
I came to understand, in a deeper way,
that I am loved infinitely and unconditionally.

This new path was not meant to be walked alone.
It is a journey we walk together—
each of us facing uncertainties in our own way,
yet choosing to move forward side by side.

Throughout the year,
God repeatedly led me into places
I did not feel fully prepared for.
Not because I was brave enough,
but because He did not allow me to remain where I was.

As I look back,
I see that the moments which truly changed me
were often marked by hesitation, inner struggle,
and obedience that did not come easily.

At times, it was standing before a new calling,
unsure whether I could carry it.
At other times, it was learning to release
the ways I once tried to stay in control.
And sometimes, it meant sitting quietly in prayer,
admitting that I did not know what came next.

I do not enjoy this kind of uncertainty.
It exposes my limitations
and removes the sense of security I once relied on.

Yet it is here that I have begun to understand:
this path is not meant to make me feel safe,
but to teach me how to be led.

And this time,
God is not leading me alone.
He is leading us—
forward.

2025 年即将结束。
回头看，我生命真正发生转向的起点，其实是在 2024 年的下半年；
但 2025 年，对我来说，是一条完全“新的路”。

这一年里，我对上帝、也对自己的生命，有了许多从前没有过的认识。
我的生命中走进了一个新的人——
他是我的另一半，我的 soulmate，
也是我最好的朋友。

他的出现，让我第一次如此真实地意识到：
上帝对我的爱并不是抽象的概念，
而是具体、丰盛、足以承载我生命的真实。
我开始明白，
我是被无限、无条件地爱着的。

这条新路，并不是我一个人走的。
它是一条我们两个人一起行走的路——
既需要各自面对前路的不确定，
也在同行中彼此确认方向。

在这一年的许多时刻，
上帝不断把我带到一些我并未完全预备好的位置。
不是因为我足够勇敢，
而是因为祂并没有让我停留在原地。

当我回头整理这些经历，
才慢慢意识到：
那些真正改变我生命的时刻，
往往伴随着迟疑、拉扯，
甚至是不情愿的顺服。

有时，是面对新的呼召，
我并不确定自己是否能承担；
有时，是在关系与选择中，
我需要放下原本习惯的掌控方式；
也有一些时候，我只能在祷告中安静下来，
承认自己并不知道下一步该往哪里去。

我并不喜欢这样的状态。
它让我清楚看见自己的有限，
也让我无法再用经验或熟练感来支撑前行。

但正是在这些时刻，
我逐渐明白，
我所走的这条路，
并不是一条让我感到安全的路，
而是一条不断被引导、被带领的路。

而这一次，
上帝并不是只带我一个人走。
祂把我们一起，
带向前方。
