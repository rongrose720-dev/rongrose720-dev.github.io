Growth Rarely Happens in the Comfort Zone

As 2025 comes to an end,
I realize that the true turning point of my life began in the second half of 2024.
But 2025 became an entirely new path.

This year reshaped how I see God and how I understand my own life.
Someone new entered my life, and I was no longer walking alone.
More importantly, God kept repeating one message to meâ€”
and only at the end of the year did I finally understand it:

Growth rarely happens in the comfort zone.

Looking back, every moment that truly stretched my life
came with a sense of discomfort.

It might be the uncertainty of answering a new calling,
the difficulty of forgiveness that goes against instinct,
or the emptiness of releasing control
without knowing what lies ahead.

Instinctively, I tried to avoid this feeling.
Discomfort feels like leaving everything familiar
and stepping into a wilderness without visible borders.
Every step raises another question.

Familiarity feels safe.
Habit feels efficient.
But this is not where He wants me to stay.

He insists on leading me outâ€”into the wilderness.
