Lonely, Yet Not Alone

Created2025-12-30|Updated2026-01-01|【Companionship & Faith】-同行与信心
|Post Views:

“Loneliness is not the absence of people, but the absence of understanding.”
孤独不仅仅是身边没有，而是缺乏真正的理解。

Lonely, Yet Not Alone

This is not a crowded path.
The road ahead is unclear,
and the answers are not revealed in advance.

In many moments,
we still have to face fear, waiting, and uncertainties on our own.
In that sense,
I am lonely on the path.

And yet—I am no longer alone.

You walk beside me,
not to carry everything for me,
not to spare me from the wilderness,
but to choose to stay
when I must keep walking.

It is a loneliness shared by two—
not the absence of one another,
but a quiet assurance in the silence:
we are still here.

And deeper still,
above and beyond our companionship,
God has been with us all along.

Not watching from a distance,
but walking among us.
When we do not know where to go,
He does.
When we feel weak,
He does not rush us.

And I understand now:
this journey continues
not because we are sufficient for one another,
but because
we are being held
by a love that is far greater than what we imagine.

既孤独，又不孤独

这是一条不热闹的路。
前方并不清晰，
答案也没有被提前揭晓。

在许多时刻，
我们仍然需要各自面对内心的恐惧、等待与不确定。
从这个意义上说，
这条路是孤独的。

但我不再是一个人了。

你走在我身旁，
不是替我承担一切，
也不是让我免于旷野，
而是在我必须行走的时候，
选择不离开。

这是一种属于两个人的孤独——
不是彼此的缺席，
而是在沉默中彼此确认：
我们仍然在这里。

而更深的一层是，
在这份陪伴之上，
上帝一直同在。

不是站在远处观看，
而是走在我们中间。
当我们不知道该往哪里去，
祂仍然知道；
当我们感到软弱，
祂并不催促。

于是我明白，
这条路之所以能走下去，
不是因为我们彼此足够，
而是因为——
我们被一份更大的爱托住。
